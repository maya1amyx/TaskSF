ABOUT TASK SF. This website was formed through the minds of four girls through Girls Who Code who had to make something (anything) for their final project. TaskSF is a website geared towards providing teenagers with jobs. These jobs can range anywhere from one-time to full-time jobs. With its easy navigation and simple, impactful design, TaskSF is a small step in stimulating the job market for teens in San Francisco.
